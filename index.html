Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG Ruling Quiz

Welcome to the Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG Ruling Quiz! It aims to help players understand rulings, and to provide a practise tool for people aiming to pass RC-1 or RC-2.

You will be successively presented some situations that could happen in a real game, described by 2 or 3 sentences. Those situations are essentially about 2 or 3 card interacting, and are written to be plausible situations.

At the end of each situation, there will be a short question about what should happen. Just beneath the questions there will be between 2 and 4 answers. There is always only one correct answer.

A button will be present on the bottom to validate your answer. You will then be given the correct answer as well as a short explanation on why it is correct. There will be also be sources when I could find pertinent ones.
